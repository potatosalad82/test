Quick Intro to GamePortals.com

We are a small student-led project put out by Potato Industries with the goal of sharing games with everyone. This is starting as a class project and we hope that it will grow into something more. We want games created by anyone big and small to be seen by the world so we want to create a database of all types of games. Anyone who follows the instructions on our home page and sends us a game will see it put up on the website with a bio to give them credit and publicity. If you want to play a game go to our games page and browse our ever growing library. *Catergories and other organization hopefully coming soon* If you don't have a game in mind try our random game page and you'll be sent a random game from our library. Also we are looking for help. We can't pay much but if this becomes big we will definitely hook you up. Anyone who has any experience in coding is welcome especially those with experience in HTML and other web development.
