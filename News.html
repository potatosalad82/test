Quick intro to GamePortals.com

We are a small student project put out by Potato Industries with the goal of sharing games with everyone. This is starting as a class project but we hope that it will grow into something more. We want to create a database of games not associated with a particular developer for gamers who want to try something new and different. So, if you want to try something new or want to post your game and get feedback we're here for you. To post your game follow the instructions on our homepage and send us a game. We will put it up with your bio. If you want to play a game go to our games page and browse our ever growing library. *Catergories and other organization hopefully coming soon* If you don't have a game in mind try our "random game page" to be sent a random game from our library. Also, we are looking for help. Anyone who has any experience in coding is welcome, especially those with experience in HTML and web developement.
